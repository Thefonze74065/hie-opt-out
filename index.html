Common Questions

Is this legal?

Yes! Most states have laws protecting your right to opt out of health information sharing while still receiving quality care.

Will this affect my care?

Healthcare providers cannot deny you care for opting out. You may need to bring medical records to new appointments.

What is Health Information Exchange (HIE)?

What HIE Does

Health Information Exchanges allow healthcare providers, hospitals, and other medical professionals to electronically share your medical records, diagnoses, medications, and visit history.

Your Privacy Rights

Most states allow you to opt out of HIE sharing while still receiving quality healthcare. Providers cannot deny you care for choosing to protect your privacy.

Why Opt Out?

Protect sensitive information from unnecessary sharing. Your dentist doesn't need to know about reproductive health visits, and your podiatrist doesn't need STI/STD history.
