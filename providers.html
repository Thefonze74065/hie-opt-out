Provider Not Listed?

If you don't see your healthcare provider in our directory, here's what you can do:

  1. Call your provider's main number and ask for the "Privacy Officer" or "HIPAA Privacy Officer"
  2. Ask specifically about "Health Information Exchange opt-out procedures"
  3. Mention Epic Care Everywhere, CareQuality, or other HIE networks by name
  4. Request written confirmation of your opt-out status

What to Say:

"I would like to opt out of all Health Information Exchange sharing. Can you please provide me with the forms and procedures to opt out of Epic Care Everywhere, CareQuality, and any other HIE networks you participate in?"

Understanding HIE Networks

Epic Care Everywhere

The largest healthcare data sharing network, connecting Epic EHR systems nationwide.

  • Coverage: 300+ health systems
  • Patients: 280+ million records
  • Opt-out: Contact your provider's Privacy Officer

Most large hospital systems use Epic and participate in Care Everywhere.

CareQuality

A national interoperability framework enabling secure health information exchange.

  • Coverage: 600+ organizations
  • Patients: 200+ million records
  • Opt-out: Provider-specific procedures

Works across different EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, etc.).

CommonWell Health Alliance

A not-for-profit alliance focused on health data interoperability.

  • Coverage: 15,000+ provider sites
  • Patients: 130+ million records
  • Opt-out: Contact participating providers

Often used by Cerner and other non-Epic systems.

State HIE Networks

Government-operated health information exchanges at the state level.

  • Examples: NC HealthConnex, SHIN-NY, CalHIE
  • Coverage: Varies by state
  • Opt-out: State-specific procedures

Check our State Guide for specific procedures.
